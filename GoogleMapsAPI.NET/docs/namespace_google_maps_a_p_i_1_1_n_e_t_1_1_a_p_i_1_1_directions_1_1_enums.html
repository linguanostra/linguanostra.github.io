Traffic model enum

Enumerator
BestGuess 

The returned duration in traffic should be the best estimate of travel time given what is known about both historical traffic conditions and live traffic

Pessimistic 

The returned duration in traffic should be longer than the actual travel time on most days, though occasional days with particularly bad traffic conditions may exceed this value.

Optimistic 

the returned duration in traffic should be shorter than the actual travel time on most days, though occasional days with particularly good traffic conditions may be faster than this value.
